Meridth, LLC is a strategic advisory firm specializing in providing technical guidance and solutions to businesses across various industries, including Fortune 500 companies. Their main services include web development, code review/security, and training in software development and technology best practices. The company aims to assist brands and startups in establishing their online presence and ensuring their technology solutions are secure and efficient. With over 20 years of industry experience, they emphasize collaboration and adaptability to meet diverse client needs.
